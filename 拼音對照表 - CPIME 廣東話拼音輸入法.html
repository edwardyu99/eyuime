拼音對照表

拼音 is a romanized Cantonese pronunciation. There are some kinds of 拼音 system in Cantonese and they are a little different in some cases. CPIME supports multi 拼音 methods: 教院式（Standard Cantonese Pinyin）, 耶魯（Yale）, 粵拼（Jyutping）, 劉錫祥（Sidney Lau）. This page shows you what is the difference among them and which 拼音 method is suitable for you.
